What is gloudio?

gloudio is a platform for audio guides – your playlist for history and culture. We believe in the power of audio. Our goal is to enrich the tourist experience by providing high quality guides produced by everyone, from experts to enthusiasts. Gloudio is a FREE app built for tourists and does not require mobile data. Simply select and download the audio guide packages you want when you have access to wifi.
